# Diligence Memo Template

Use this structure by default. Compress it for a quick screen.

## 1. Recommendation

- One-sentence conclusion
- Decision status: proceed, proceed with conditions, hold, or pass
- Confidence level: high, medium, or low

## 2. Company Snapshot

- What the company does
- Who it sells to
- Stage, geography, and business model

## 3. Thesis Summary

- Core reasons the company could be attractive
- What appears differentiated
- Why now may matter

## 4. Key Risks

- Top 3 to 5 risks
- Severity and likelihood
- What evidence supports each risk

## 5. Workstream Findings

Cover only the relevant sections:

- Business and market
- Product and technology
- Commercial traction
- Financial quality
- Legal and compliance
- Management and governance

For each section, use this pattern:

- Finding
- Why it matters
- Evidence
- Confidence

## 6. Open Questions

- List unanswered items that could change the decision
- Note what data or conversations would resolve them

## 7. Next Diligence Steps

- Immediate asks
- Nice-to-have follow-ups
- Specialists needed, if any
